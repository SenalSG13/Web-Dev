-- Inline Elements --

** Do not start on a new line.
** Take only the necessary width.
EX: * span, * img, * a

-- Block level Elements --

** Start on a new line.
** Take full width available.
EX: *div, * h1 - h6, * p, * form

Singer/songwriter Taylor Swift was earning renown as a country music singer by the age of 16.
